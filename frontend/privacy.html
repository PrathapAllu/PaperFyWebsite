StepDoc Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to StepDoc! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our website, app, and related services. Please read it carefully to understand your rights and our responsibilities.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account or log in.
  • Social Login Data: Public profile information (such as name and email) if you use Google or other social login options.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and actions taken on our website and app.
  • Device & Technical Data: Device type, browser, operating system, and IP address.
  • Support & Communication: Information you provide when contacting support or submitting requests.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain StepDoc services.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our offerings.
  • To communicate with you about your account, updates, and support requests.
  • To ensure security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.
  • To analyze usage and improve our website and app.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in these cases:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • With trusted service providers who help us operate StepDoc (under strict confidentiality).
  • To comply with laws, regulations, or legal requests.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of StepDoc, our users, or others.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Please protect your account credentials and contact us if you suspect any unauthorized activity.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your account information at any time.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request information about how your data is used or shared.
  • Contact us for privacy-related requests at support@stepdoc.com.

7. Children's Privacy

StepDoc is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest update date will always be shown at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated to you via our website or email.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at support@stepdoc.com.
